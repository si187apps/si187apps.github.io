How it works

Four coaching tools.
One consistent philosophy.

Captain analysis
The right captain pick. Explained.
Every gameweek, we model expected returns for your captaincy options across the next 3 gameweeks — not just this one. Fixture difficulty, home/away splits, recent form, and ownership context all factored in.
Ceiling vs. floor · differential value · ownership risk
FDR adjusted for opponent form · blank/DGW context
Transfer impact
Compelling, marginal, or trap.
We classify every potential transfer across three categories — so you know at a glance whether a move is genuinely worth making, borderline noise, or a week-5 FPL classic trap that will haunt you.
3-gameweek projected points delta · price change risk
Bench coverage impact · template vs. differential trade-off
Formation optimiser
Your best XI, with alternatives.
Given your squad, which formation maximises expected points this week — and what are you trading off by choosing it? We model your top 2–3 lineup options side by side so you can see what each one costs.
Expected points per formation · bench order impact
Injury/suspension risk flagging · differential scenarios
Chip strategy
When to play your chips.
Wildcard, Triple Captain, Free Hit, Bench Boost — each chip has an optimal window. We track the season-wide fixture calendar and flag when conditions are aligned for each chip, so you don't waste your Wildcard on a bad week.
DGW and BGW tracking · fixture swing windows
Template chip timing vs. contrarian plays
Transfer classification — how it works
★ Compelling
Clear points advantage over 3 gameweeks. Fixture swing in favour. Marginal cost justified. Make the transfer.
→ Strong signal to act
◎ Marginal
Within statistical noise — could go either way. The data doesn't support paying a premium for this. Hold unless you have a free hit.
→ Hold unless free transfer
✗ Trap
Popular move with weak underlying rationale. High ownership pressure, low points ceiling. The kind of transfer you regret by Sunday night.
→ Resist the template

Thursday night
Who should I captain this week?
Fixture context, expected returns, ownership differential, ceiling vs. floor — laid out clearly so you can make the call with confidence before the deadline.
Transfer window
Is this transfer worth a −4?
We model the 3-gameweek points swing, flag whether the points premium justifies the hit, and tell you if the popular move is a trap. Compelling, marginal, or trap — clearly labelled.
Chip decisions
When should I play my Wildcard?
We track the fixture calendar all season and flag the optimal windows for each chip. No more wasting your Triple Captain on a blank gameweek because you panicked.
DGW / BGW weeks
How do I navigate blank and double gameweeks?
BGWs and DGWs swing seasons. We analyse your squad coverage and suggest the clearest path through — hold, Free Hit, or restructure — with the rationale explained.
Lineup selection
What's my best XI this week?
Given your squad, injuries, and this week's fixtures, we give you your best formation options — including what you're trading off with each one.
Season strategy
Am I building for now or the run-in?
The best FPL squads are structured around fixture swings. We help you understand whether your current squad is optimised for the next 3 gameweeks or the next 10 — and what that trade-off costs.
