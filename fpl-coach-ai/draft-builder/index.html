How we picked this squad

This squad is built from proven Premier League performers — players with at least one full season of FPL data. Our model uses expected goals, expected assists, fixture difficulty, and form to predict GW1 returns.

New Premier League signings from abroad — players making their PL debut in 2026/27 — are not included. Without match data from the Premier League specifically, any prediction would be a guess. We only recommend players our model can honestly evaluate.

Pre-season form doesn't feature in our model. Friendlies and pre-season tournaments don't predict GW1 returns reliably. We weight actual Premier League performance data instead.
