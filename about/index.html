Origin

Why si187 exists

Most apps are built for the market.
We build for the user.

si187 Apps started from a straightforward frustration: the apps that dominate the charts are optimised for engagement metrics, subscription conversion, and investor decks — not for the people who use them every day. They're bloated, they're noisy, and they're designed to keep you opening the app rather than actually helping you.

The first problem we chose to solve was Fantasy Premier League. Eleven million people managing FPL squads every gameweek, and the tools available to them fell into two useless camps: raw data dumps that required a statistics degree to interpret, or black-box "AI" that just told you what to do and offered no reasoning. Neither approach respected the intelligence of the manager.

So we built FPL Coach AI. Not an oracle. Not a stats wall. A coaching companion — something that surfaces the right information at the right moment and trusts you to make the call. That philosophy, coach not oracle, is the founding idea that runs through everything si187 builds.

What we believe

The si187
way of building.

01
One thing at a time
We don't build ten mediocre apps. We pick one problem, understand it properly, build the best possible solution, and learn everything we can before moving to the next. Breadth is the enemy of quality.
02
The user owns the outcome
Our apps don't make decisions on your behalf. They surface information, flag trade-offs, and explain the reasoning — then step back. You're smarter than an algorithm that just tells you what to do. We build tools that respect that.
03
Honest about uncertainty
When the data is genuinely inconclusive, we say so. When a recommendation is within statistical noise, we tell you. False confidence is worse than no confidence. Our products tell you what they don't know as clearly as what they do.
04
Accountable to users, not investors
No VC round means no pressure to chase engagement metrics over genuine utility. We charge a fair price for something genuinely useful. If it's not useful enough to pay for, we haven't built it right yet.
Our commitment

How we work
with you.

We don't disappear after launch. The apps we build get better because the people using them tell us where the gaps are, what's working, and what isn't. Here's what that looks like in practice.

Regular feature drops
We ship improvements on a rolling basis. No big annual updates. Fixes, refinements, and new capabilities based on what testers and users ask for — fast.
Transparent roadmap
What's coming, why it's coming, and when we expect it — all visible on our public roadmap. If priorities change, we explain why. No black box planning.
Direct access
No ticket system. No support queue. Email studio@si187apps.com and you'll hear back from someone who actually built the thing. We read everything and respond to everyone who makes the effort.
Market testing done right
Before we build the next thing, we listen. We run structured validation through our Labs programme — real conversations with real users before a line of code gets written.
No dark patterns
We don't use artificial scarcity, guilt messaging, manipulative notifications, or engagement traps. If you use our app, it's because it's useful — not because we tricked you into it.
Building in public
We share our decisions, our reasoning, and our trade-offs as we go. Follow along on X, Instagram, and TikTok — @si187apps. The process is part of the product.
