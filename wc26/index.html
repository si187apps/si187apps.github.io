Why this exists

The World Cup deserves a companion built for fans, not organisations.

Most football apps treat you as a generic user. WC26: The Journey treats you as a fan of a specific nation — with everything, from the colours to the news feed to the lineup builder, built around who you're supporting.

✗ Generic tournament apps

The same experience for every fan regardless of who they support. Fixtures buried, no personalisation, no sense of your team's journey — just the raw tournament data with nowhere to put yourself in it.

✓ WC26: The Journey

Pick England and it turns red and white. Pick Brazil and it turns green and gold. Your squad, your news, your lineup — personalised to the nation you're backing, from the first tap to the Final.

How it works

Set up in under 60 seconds. Personal from the first tap.

01

Pick your nation

Choose from all 48 qualified nations. The moment you tap, the whole app transforms to their colours — a full theme shift in under half a second. Your World Cup journey starts here.

02

Explore your squad

Browse the full World Cup squad, grouped by position. Tap any player to see their tournament stats — appearances, goals, assists, and ratings. Then build your own predicted XI from the same squad list.

03

Follow the wall chart

Track your nation's route through the bracket — from group stage to knockout rounds. The Journey Tracker highlights every step in your team's colours. Every result auto-populates.

04

Add fixtures and get alerts

One tap adds all your team's remaining matches to your phone calendar with kick-off alerts. Goal notifications arrive within seconds. You'll never miss a moment.

05

If your team goes out, follow another

When your nation is eliminated, the app surfaces a moment to pick a second team. New colours. New journey. New reason to keep watching all the way to the Final.

In the app

What the app actually does for you.

Match day
Where is the game, what time is kick-off, and who's starting? Your next fixture is on the home screen. Predicted lineup, venue, time local to you. One tap adds it to your calendar with a 1-hour alert.
Squad debate
Who would you start? Who's in form? Browse the full squad with player stats from the tournament — goals, assists, ratings. Build your predicted XI, pick your formation, and share it as a branded card in your nation's colours.
Between matches
What's being written about your team right now? The news feed filters five live sources to your nation first — manager press conferences, injury updates, player ratings. Always the latest story at the top.
Live game
You're away from the TV. What's the score? A persistent live bar sits at the top of every screen. Goal alerts push to your phone within seconds. Scorers, stats, and the full match timeline in the match centre.
Your team's out
The tournament isn't over. Who do you back now? The app surfaces a team-switch moment when your nation is eliminated. Pick a new team, theme pivots, journey tracker updates. Stay in it to the Final.
