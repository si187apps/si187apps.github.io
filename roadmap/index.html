Our process

How we decide what
gets built next.

We don't pick the next app based on market size reports or investor interest. We build from a specific question: is there a group of people who need this badly enough that they'd tell us unprompted?

01
Listen first, always
Ideas don't come from brainstorms. They come from hearing the same frustration from enough different people. We track patterns in what our community tells us — through Labs, through support, through social.
02
Validate before building
Before writing a line of code, we talk to potential users. Not "would you use this?" — that question is useless. Instead: "tell us about the last time you struggled with this." Real stories, not hypothetical preferences.
03
The si187 filter
The idea has to pass our internal filter: is the existing solution genuinely bad, or just imperfect? Can we build something meaningfully better — not marginally better? Is there a clear "coach not oracle" version of this product?
